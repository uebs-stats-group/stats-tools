**Topic:** Claude Code × Stata & Python: Building a Domain-Specific AI Research Agent<br>
**Speaker:** Runzhi Tian, Yifan Qi<br>
**Location:** UEBS Boardroom<br>
**Time:** 27/03/2026 17:00 – 18:00 (GMT)<br>
**Abstract:** AI coding assistants like Claude Code can write, execute, and debug code autonomously — but they don't work with  Stata & Python out of the box. Getting them to talk to each other requires a deliberate configuration pipeline that most economists have never encountered.

This hands-on workshop guides participants through the full setup process step by step, so that everyone leaves the session with a working AI-powered Stata &Python environment on their own machine. We will also share practical tips from real research use, followed by open Q&A.

Bring your laptop and your Anthropic account — we'll get it running together.

How to use AI in Research — part of the UEBS Statistics Study Group series bridging traditional econometrics and AI-powered research workflows.
